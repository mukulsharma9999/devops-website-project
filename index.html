// Who We Are
ICNova Electronics

ICNova Electronics Pvt. Ltd. is a Ghaziabad-based import-export company specialising in premium LED lighting systems, architectural track lights, housing lights, and precision electronic components.

We bridge global electronics manufacturers with Indian businesses. Our catalogue spans LED bulbs, panels, reflector track lights (ECO & Premium), foot lights, way lights, and every key component — MOSFETs, capacitors, IC chips, transistors, resistors, and LED drivers.

We source directly from verified manufacturers in China, Taiwan, and South Korea — ensuring authenticity, competitive pricing, and consistent quality for all our B2B clients across India.
